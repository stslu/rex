enum GridSummaryType
flags SummaryTypes

The enumerator of possible summary types that can be processed by the grid.

ConstantValueDescription
SummaryNone0Summaries are not supported by the binding.
SummarySum0x0001Summary (sum).
SummaryMin0x0002Summary (minimal value).
SummaryMax0x0004Summary (maximum value).
SummaryCount0x0008Summary (total count).
SummaryAvg0x0010Summary (average).

The SummaryTypes type is a typedef for QFlags<GridSummaryType>. It stores an OR combination of GridSummaryType values.
